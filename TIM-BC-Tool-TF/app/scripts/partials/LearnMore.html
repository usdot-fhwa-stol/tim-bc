This TIM Task Force (TIM-TF) Benefit-Cost Module was developed by FHWA to assist local, State, and Federal governments or agencies with assessing the total value of an TIM-TF program. TIM-TFs are groups of TIM-related planners, managers, and other personnel that meet periodically to coordinate activities and policies. TIM-TF programs are effective methods for managing traffic incidents to reduce traffic delays, wasted fuel, emissions, and the incidence of secondary crashes. This tool calculates program costs and benefits, in terms of monetized equivalents of the measures of effectiveness listed above, and provides an overall benefit/cost ratio of the program. The tool can be used to assess an expansion of an existing TIM-TF program, assess the value of creating a new TIM-TF program, or compare alternatives.
