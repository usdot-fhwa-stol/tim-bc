ABOUT FHWA TIM-BC TOOL

Current Version: 3.0


The TIM-BC tool is a web-based software with standardized BC methodologies that can be universally employed in BC analysis of different TIM strategies. With its built-in databases and empirical equations, the tool is simple to use and less data-intensive. This tool was developed by contractors and researchers using funding from the Federal Highway Administration (FHWA), part of the United States Department of Transportation (USDOT).


For more information about the TIM-BC tool, please visit the FHWA TIM-BC Confluence space.


Please report any questions, feedback, or issues to the Saxton Transportation Operations Laboratory at Saxtonlab.FHWA@dot.gov.
